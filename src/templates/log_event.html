How Usage Is Calculated

Usage is automatically derived from the difference between consecutive stock events. For example, if you logged 10 units at end-of-day on Monday and 8 units on Tuesday, the system will calculate 2 units of usage on Tuesday. This is more accurate than manual entry and helps us detect spillage or waste.
